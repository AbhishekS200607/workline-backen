Frequently Asked Questions

How do I book a service?

Simply select a service category, choose your preferred professional, select date and time, and confirm your booking. You can pay online or choose cash on service.

Are all workers verified?

Yes, all our professionals undergo background verification, skill assessment, and document verification before joining our platform.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept UPI payments, credit/debit cards, digital wallets, and cash on service completion.

Can I reschedule or cancel my booking?

Yes, you can reschedule or cancel your booking up to 2 hours before the scheduled time through your dashboard.

What if I'm not satisfied with the service?

We offer 100% satisfaction guarantee. Contact our support team within 24 hours, and we'll resolve the issue or provide a refund.
